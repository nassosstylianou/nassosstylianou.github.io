Nassos Stylianou

Stories, analysis and data visualisation on climate change and the clean energy transition at Global Energy Monitor. I specialise in finding stories from complex datasets and visualising them in clear and compelling ways. Formerly a senior data journalist at BBC News, where I was editorial lead on a number of the most successful and impactful BBC projects. My work often included complicated data analysis and used innovative storytelling formats and techniques, receiving recognition at prestigious industry awards.
